Additive Synthesis

  • Additive synthesis is a technique that creates waveforms by adding sine waves together.
  • Any sound can be made by combining a series of sine waves at various frequencies and amplitudes to create a new, harmonically-rich waveform.
  • The Wave Generator above can further explain this by allowing the user to visualize what happens to a fundamental frequency when other sine waves are added to it at various harmonic intervals and amplitudes.
  • START BY MAKING SURE YOUR COMPUTER'S SPEAKER VOLUME IS SET TO A LOW LEVEL!
